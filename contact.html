Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a 3D file?

Not necessarily! If you have a physical object, we can 3D scan it. If you have an idea, we can help with design consultation or refer you to designers.

What file formats do you accept?

We work with STL, OBJ, 3MF, and other common 3D file formats. Not sure? Send us what you have and we'll let you know.

How long does printing take?

It varies by project size and complexity. Small items may take a few hours, while larger or detailed prints can take 24+ hours. We'll provide timeline estimates with your quote.

What are your size limits?

Our print bed accommodates objects up to [specify dimensions]. For larger projects, we can print in sections and assemble.

Can you ship prints?

Yes! We can carefully package and ship your completed prints. Shipping costs will be added to your quote.

Do you offer design services?

We focus on printing and scanning. For design work, we can recommend trusted CAD designers who work with our clients.
