3D Printing Services

Transform your digital designs into physical reality with our professional 3D printing services.

What We Print

  • Replacement Parts: Reproduce discontinued or hard-to-find components
  • Custom Designs: Bring your unique ideas to life
  • Prototypes: Test and iterate your designs quickly
  • Small Production Runs: Create multiples of your parts
  • Decorative Items: Custom home decor and personalized gifts

Materials & Finishes

  • PLA (Polylactic Acid) - Eco-friendly, great for general use
  • ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) - Durable and heat-resistant
  • PETG - Strong, flexible, and weather-resistant
  • Multiple color options available
  • Post-processing and finishing services

Our Process

  1. Consultation: Discuss your project requirements and goals
  2. Design Review: Analyze your files, help with 3D scanning, or create custom designs
  3. Quote: Receive detailed pricing and timeline
  4. Production: Watch your project come to life
  5. Delivery: Receive your finished parts

3D Scanning Services

Capture the physical world digitally with precision 3D scanning technology.

Scanning Applications

  • Reverse Engineering: Create digital models of existing objects
  • Quality Control: Compare manufactured parts to original specs
  • Digital Archiving: Preserve artifacts and historical items
  • Reproduction: Scan objects for accurate 3D printing
  • Design Reference: Capture real-world dimensions for CAD work

What We Can Scan

  • Small to medium-sized objects (up to 12 inches)
  • Complex geometries and intricate details
  • Various materials and surface finishes
  • Multiple objects for batch projects

Deliverables

  • High-resolution 3D mesh files (STL, OBJ)
  • Editable CAD files (optional)
  • Color texture maps (when applicable)
  • Technical documentation and measurements
